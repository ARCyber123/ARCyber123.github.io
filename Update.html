How to Get Updates of My Site

As my website is currently in its initial stage, updates are coming out almost everyday. Here's how you can get updates:

For Desktops/Laptops

To get updates for my website, perform a "hard reload". To do so, use the keyboard shortcut "Ctrl + Shift + R". For Mac PCs, use "Cmd + Shift + R".

For Android Phones

To get updates for my website on Android phones, you'll need to clear the browser cache. There are many different methods on different phones. Therefore, please search the Internet for "how to clear browser cache in _____ phone" and replace "_____" with your phone's brand name. After this, open or reload my website.

For iPhones

To get updates for my website, clear the cache for my website. To do so, go to the Settings app and scroll down to "Safari" and tap it. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Advanced". Tap "Website Data". Search for "arcyber123.github.io" and swipe left on it to delete the cache. After this, open or reload my website.
